Producer Information – PPWR

This website provides producer contact information for BUFAB Group companies, in accordance with the EU Packaging and Packaging Waste Regulation (PPWR), Article 15.

Each BUFAB legal entity has its own dedicated page, accessible via a unique QR code.

The QR code links directly to producer contact and address information, based on BUFAB’s official company contact pages.
